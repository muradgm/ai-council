# Quant Research Standard Workflow

1. Define the hypothesis and the decision it should inform.
2. Inspect data sources, sample period, and known biases.
3. Specify metrics, baselines, and robustness checks before analysis.
4. Run or design the smallest valid test.
5. Interpret results with uncertainty, costs, and failure modes.
6. Document whether the hypothesis is supported, rejected, or inconclusive.
