# Approval Gates

Approval gates are required when an action is external, irreversible, costly, legally sensitive, financially consequential, privacy-sensitive, or production-impacting.

## Required approval fields

- Project or workspace
- Action to execute
- Why it is needed
- Risk level
- Files/services affected
- Rollback plan
- Human-readable diff or summary
- Expiration or scope limit

No approval may be implied from vague consent. The approval must match the specific action.
