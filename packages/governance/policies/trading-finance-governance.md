# Trading and Finance Governance

Trading content is high-risk by default.

## Allowed

- Educational explanations
- Journal templates
- Risk dashboards
- Backtesting methodology
- Software architecture
- General risk management concepts

## Not allowed without strict reframing

- “Buy/sell now” decisions
- Personalized position sizing
- Profit guarantees
- Hidden-risk marketing
- Broker-rule evasion
- Live-trading automation without human approval and safety controls

## Required language

Trading tools must be framed as analysis, journaling, and risk-support systems, not profit machines.
