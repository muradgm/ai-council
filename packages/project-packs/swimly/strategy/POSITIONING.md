# Positioning: Swimly

## Category

decision-first pool recommender.

## Primary Audience

Berlin swimmers, families, fitness swimmers, budget-sensitive users, people choosing between nearby pools.

## Positioning Sentence

Swimly helps Berlin swimmers solve a practical workflow problem by focusing on a simple recommender that picks the best pool option based on purpose, district, price, and practical fit instead of overwhelming users with a long list.

## Messaging Principles

- Say what the product does in concrete language.
- Avoid vague AI claims.
- Explain the practical outcome.
- Show how the product reduces decision friction.
- Keep the wedge visible.
