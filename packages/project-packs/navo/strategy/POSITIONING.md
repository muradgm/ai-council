# Positioning: NAVO / Flowday

## Category

route-aware city trip planning app.

## Primary Audience

families on city trips, weekend travelers, business travelers with free time, local explorers.

## Positioning Sentence

NAVO / Flowday helps families on city trips solve a practical workflow problem by focusing on turns scattered city ideas into a practical route-aware day plan for families, short visits, and free-time exploration.

## Messaging Principles

- Say what the product does in concrete language.
- Avoid vague AI claims.
- Explain the practical outcome.
- Show how the product reduces decision friction.
- Keep the wedge visible.
