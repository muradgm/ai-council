# Project Pack: NAVO / Flowday

## Product Type

route-aware city trip planning app.

## Product Wedge

Turns scattered city ideas into a practical route-aware day plan for families, short visits, and free-time exploration.

## Current Stage

prototype / travel planner evolution.

## Target Users

- families on city trips
- weekend travelers
- business travelers with free time
- local explorers

## Core Problem

Travel planning tools often show attractions as lists or maps, but users still need to decide what fits together by time, location, opening hours, transport, and child-friendliness.

## MVP Scope

- activity discovery
- like/save activity
- route-aware day planner
- kid-friendly filters
- weather-aware notes
- budget/cost hints
- Google Maps handoff

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- Keep the product route-aware, not booking-first.
- Prioritize mobile-first planning speed.
- Make generated plans editable by the user.
- Represent practical uncertainty clearly.
