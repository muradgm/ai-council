# Positioning: SignalScout

## Category

AI research, audit, outreach, and opportunity pipeline.

## Primary Audience

solo founders, small agencies, technical consultants, outreach-heavy service businesses.

## Positioning Sentence

SignalScout helps solo founders solve a practical workflow problem by focusing on a minimal revenue system for finding, qualifying, auditing, and contacting businesses with clear improvement opportunities.

## Messaging Principles

- Say what the product does in concrete language.
- Avoid vague AI claims.
- Explain the practical outcome.
- Show how the product reduces decision friction.
- Keep the wedge visible.
