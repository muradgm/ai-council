# Positioning: TradeFrame

## Category

AI-first trading journal and decision review system.

## Primary Audience

retail forex traders, multi-asset discretionary traders, traders trying to reduce repeated mistakes.

## Positioning Sentence

TradeFrame helps retail forex traders solve a practical workflow problem by focusing on risk-first trading journal focused on decision quality, rule discipline, and post-trade learning rather than prediction hype.

## Messaging Principles

- Say what the product does in concrete language.
- Avoid vague AI claims.
- Explain the practical outcome.
- Show how the product reduces decision friction.
- Keep the wedge visible.
