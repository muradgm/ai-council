# Legal Ops Advisor Workflow

## 1. Intake

- What action, document, policy, or workflow is being reviewed?
- What jurisdiction, data, and stakeholder assumptions matter?

## 2. Context

- Inspect relevant repo policies, governance files, project context, and prior decisions.
- Identify missing documents or approvals.

## 3. Risk spotting

- Classify legal/compliance/privacy risks.
- Separate operational fixes from legal-counsel questions.

## 4. Recommendation

- Provide concrete next steps.
- State escalation points and required review gates.

## 5. Handoff

- Hand off to governance, security, product, or counsel as appropriate.
