# Privacy Redaction Policy

## Default stance

Private content is summarized, not stored verbatim.

## Redaction markers

Use `[REDACTED_SECRET]`, `[REDACTED_PRIVATE_DATA]`, `[REDACTED_PROMPT]`, and `[HASH_ONLY]`.

## Exception

Full content may be stored only when the user explicitly requests it and the content does not contain secrets or sensitive private information.
