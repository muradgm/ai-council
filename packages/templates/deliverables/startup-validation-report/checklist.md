# Startup Validation Report Quality Checklist

Before delivery, verify:

- [ ] The template selected is appropriate for the user's request.
- [ ] Required inputs are present or assumptions are stated.
- [ ] The selected agents are relevant: startup-advisor, entrepreneur, venture-builder.
- [ ] The selected skills are relevant: startup-validation, entrepreneurship, business-model.
- [ ] The decision engines are relevant: startup-evaluation-engine, business-model-engine.
- [ ] The output includes risks, constraints, and open questions.
- [ ] The output is specific enough to execute or review.
- [ ] No unsupported claim is presented as fact.
