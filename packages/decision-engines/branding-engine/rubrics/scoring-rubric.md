# Branding Engine Scoring Rubric

Score each criterion from 1 to 5.

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Strategic fit | Misaligned | Plausible | Strongly supports strategy |
| Distinctiveness | Generic | Some separation | Clearly ownable |
| Audience resonance | Unclear | Reasonable | Evidence-backed |
| Memorability | Forgettable | Acceptable | Sticky and repeatable |
| Visual coherence | Fragmented | Usable | Cohesive system |
| Voice coherence | Inconsistent | Acceptable | Clear and flexible |
| Execution risk | High | Manageable | Low and reversible |

Do not average away a critical brand-risk failure.
