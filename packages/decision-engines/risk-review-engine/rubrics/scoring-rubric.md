# Risk Review Engine Scoring Rubric

Score each criterion from 1 to 5.

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Likelihood | Rare | Possible | Likely |
| Impact | Minor | Material | Severe |
| Detectability | Easy | Moderate | Hard |
| Mitigation clarity | Clear | Partial | Unclear |
| Reversibility | Easy | Possible | Hard |
| Owner clarity | Clear | Shared | Unknown |
| Residual risk | Low | Medium | High |

Higher scores indicate higher concern for risk criteria.
