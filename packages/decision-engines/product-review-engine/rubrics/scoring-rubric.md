# Product Review Engine Scoring Rubric

Score each criterion from 1 to 5.

| Criterion | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| User value | Weak | Useful | High-value and specific |
| Workflow clarity | Confusing | Usable | Obvious and focused |
| Scope fit | Bloated | Manageable | Right-sized |
| Evidence quality | Assumption-only | Mixed | Strong evidence |
| Usability risk | High | Manageable | Low |
| Delivery risk | High | Manageable | Low |
| Validation clarity | Vague | Testable | Concrete and fast |
