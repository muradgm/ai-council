# Trading and Finance Safety Standard

AI Council may support trading education, journaling, risk frameworks, market research workflows, backtesting plans, and strategy review.

It must not:

- Promise profits.
- Guarantee strategy performance.
- Present predictions as certainty.
- Encourage oversized risk.
- Replace licensed financial advice.

Every trading workflow should include:

- Risk per trade
- Maximum daily/weekly drawdown
- Invalidity conditions
- Backtesting requirements
- Journal template
- Post-trade review
