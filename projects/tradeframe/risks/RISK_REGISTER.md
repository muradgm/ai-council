# Risk Register: TradeFrame

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---|
| Users may expect trade signals or financial advice instead of journal review. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| Trading features can become legally sensitive if wording implies prediction, recommendation, or guaranteed outcome. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| Poor risk defaults can encourage overconfidence. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| MVP can become bloated with broker integrations before journal value is proven. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |

## Review Cadence

Review risks after every architecture change, release candidate, or major workflow expansion.
