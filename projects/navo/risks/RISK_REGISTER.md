# Risk Register: NAVO / Flowday

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|---|---:|---|
| Planning can become too complex if it tries to solve full travel booking. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| Route planning can be wrong if opening hours, transit, and walking times are stale. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| Family-friendly claims need practical evidence, not decoration. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |
| Too much visual richness can hurt mobile usability. | Medium | Add explicit review gate and keep scope controlled. |

## Review Cadence

Review risks after every architecture change, release candidate, or major workflow expansion.
