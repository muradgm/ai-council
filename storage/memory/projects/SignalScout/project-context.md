# Project Context: SignalScout

## Purpose

AI research, audit, outreach, and opportunity pipeline.

## Product Wedge

A minimal revenue system for finding, qualifying, auditing, and contacting businesses with clear improvement opportunities.

## Current Stage

monorepo / pipeline design.

## Target Users

- solo founders
- small agencies
- technical consultants
- outreach-heavy service businesses

## Core Problem

Opportunity research and outreach are scattered across scraping, spreadsheets, manual audits, and generic messages that do not connect to a clear revenue workflow.

## MVP Scope

- lead source ingestion
- website audit
- opportunity scoring
- outreach draft
- CRM-style pipeline
- audit report export
- follow-up reminders

## Non-Negotiable Constraints

- Respect robots, privacy, anti-spam, and platform terms.
- Keep outreach reviewable by a human before sending.
- Make audit criteria explainable.
- Optimize for one sellable workflow before expanding.
